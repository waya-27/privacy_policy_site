NiceShot Log Privacy Policy

Language: English (See Japanese)

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Information collected

  • Golf practice data (shot records, distance, club type, etc.)
  • Course information (practice location, date and time, weather, etc.)
  • Statistical data (average distance, accuracy, progress status, etc.)
  • App usage status (screen transitions, frequency of feature usage, etc.)
  • Device information (OS, device type, app version)
  • Information necessary for ad delivery such as advertising identifiers
  • App crash information and error logs

Method of collection

  • Direct input by the user
  • Automatic collection by the app (SDKs for analysis, advertising, crash analysis, etc.)

2. Purpose of Use

  • Provision of golf practice record management and statistical analysis functions
  • Visualization of practice progress and support for performance improvement
  • Analysis for service improvement (UI/UX improvement, bug fixes, etc.)
  • Ad delivery and effectiveness measurement
  • Support and inquiry response

3. Sharing and Provision of Information

Provision to third parties

In principle, we do not provide personal information to third parties.

Exceptional provision

  • When required by law
  • When there is user consent

4. Information Protection

Security measures

  • Data encryption
  • Appropriate access control
  • Periodic security audits

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

This app may use the following technologies:

  • Saving in-app settings
  • Optimization of ad delivery (use of advertising identifiers, etc.)

6. Use of External Services

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) — Privacy Policy

7. User Rights

  • Right to view data
  • Right to delete data
  • Right to correct data

8. Data Retention Period

  • Practice data: Until app deletion
  • Log data: In principle, 1 year

9. Account Deletion

9.1 How to delete

Users can delete their account and all data by the following method:

  1. Open the app
  2. Open the settings screen
  3. Select "Delete Data"
  4. Tap "Delete" on the confirmation screen

9.2 Data to be deleted

The following data will be completely deleted by account deletion:

  • All shot records
  • All course information
  • Practice history
  • Statistical data
  • User settings

9.3 Notes

  • Deleted data cannot be restored
  • The deletion process is executed immediately
  • The app will not be usable after deletion
