Arealist Privacy Policy

Language: English (See Japanese)

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Information collected

  • Location information (GPS coordinates, area boundary data)
  • Activity records (visited places, dates and times, duration of stay, etc.)
  • Map data (custom markers, area information)
  • Photos/Image data (images associated with areas)
  • Device information (OS, device type, app version)
  • Information necessary for ad delivery such as advertising identifiers
  • App usage status (screen transitions, frequency of feature usage, etc.)
  • App crash information and error logs

Method of collection

  • Direct input by the user
  • Automatic acquisition by GPS/location services
  • Image loading from camera/photo library
  • Automatic collection by the app (SDKs for analysis, advertising, crash analysis, etc.)

2. Use of Location Information

This app uses location information as a main feature:

  • Displaying current location and checking on the map
  • Automatic detection and recording of areas
  • History management of visited areas
  • Boundary setting of custom areas

Timing of location acquisition

  • Acquired only while using the app (we do not acquire in the background)
  • Only when the user enables the location information function
  • Use of location information can be disabled from settings at any time

3. Purpose of Use

  • Providing area management and recording functions
  • Providing map display and navigation functions
  • Management and visualization of activity history
  • Analysis for service improvement (UI/UX improvement, bug fixes, etc.)
  • Ad delivery and effectiveness measurement
  • Support and inquiry response

4. Sharing and Provision of Information

Provision to third parties

In principle, we do not provide personal information to third parties. In particular, location information is saved only on the device and is not sent to external servers.

Exceptional provision

  • When required by law
  • When there is user consent

5. Information Protection

Security measures

  • Data encryption
  • Appropriate access control
  • Secure storage in local storage

6. Camera and Photo Library Permissions

This app requests access to the camera and photo library for the following purposes:

  • Taking photos related to areas
  • Associating existing photos with areas
  • Photo data is saved only on the device and is not sent externally
  • Permissions are not used unless these functions are used

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

This app may use the following technologies:

  • Saving in-app settings
  • Optimization of ad delivery (use of advertising identifiers, etc.)
  • Improvement of user experience

8. Use of External Services

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) - Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap (Map data provision) - Privacy Policy
  • Flutter Map (Map display library) - Open source library

9. User Rights

  • Right to view data
  • Right to delete data
  • Right to correct data
  • Right to stop using location information

10. Data Retention Period

  • Location information/Area data: Until app deletion
  • Photo data: Until deleted by the user
  • Log data: In principle, 1 year

11. Account Deletion

11.1 How to delete

Users can delete their account and all data by the following method:

  1. Open the app
  2. Open the settings screen
  3. Select "Delete Data"
  4. Tap "Delete" on the confirmation screen

11.2 Data to be deleted

The following data will be completely deleted by account deletion:

  • All location information records
  • All area data
  • Activity history
  • Associated photo data
  • User settings

11.3 Notes

  • Deleted data cannot be restored
  • The deletion process is executed immediately
  • The app will not be usable after deletion
