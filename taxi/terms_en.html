Article 2 (Service Overview)

The Service is an application that facilitates taxi dispatch requests and matching for nursing care taxis. The main functions are as follows:

  • Dispatch Request: Function to request a taxi by specifying the user's location, pickup/drop-off points, and date/time.
  • Matching: Function to match users with drivers.
  • Location Information Function: Display of current location and nearby vehicle positions (based on device location permission).
  • Notification Function: Delivery of notifications regarding dispatch status, etc.
